About the author

David Alexandru - a primary school boy living in the UK - is the mastermind who wrote and designed the Country Balls comic book series. 

His collection of comics features personified national flags depicted as spherical characters known as Country Balls, and experiencing chaotic adventures involving time travel, extraterrestrial invasions, and global conflicts.

He was 10 years old when he wrote his first comic called "Country Balls", followed by "Country Balls Return" and "Country Balls Heroes".

"Country Balls Golden Era" is a 15-chapter collection that gathers the wildest comic adventures combining the content of his three books: "Country Balls", "Country Balls Return", and "Country Balls Heroes" - all in one volume.

At 11 years old he authored "Country Balls Mania".

Throughout the series, the author utilises humour and personification to illustrate historical rivalries, sporting events, and imaginative adventures. The visual style is characterised by vibrant colours and simple dialogue bubbles to convey action and suspense within each short story.
