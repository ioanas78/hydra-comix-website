The Collection

Country Balls

Ever wondered what would happen if the entire world turned into round, bouncy balls with major attitude problems? Welcome to COUNTRY BALLS, the ultimate collection of wild stories.

Whether they are fighting over fishing spots, surviving detention, or discovering dragons in deep space, these Country Balls are never boring. Perfect for kids aged 8+ who want non-stop laughs, suspense, and action!

Country Balls Return

The world's roundest heroes face total madness! Poland survives inter-dimensional portals, dragon battles, and a very awkward love triangle. Italy gets possessed by a demon at a spooky castle — saved only by loud music. Greece encounters a terrifying underwater monster, and the Skull King launches a demon invasion while the UN argues. To save the day, the countries must merge into one giant super-unit and throw the planet Mercury at their enemies!

Country Balls Heroes

Forget everything you know about countries — these balls are Forces of Nature! When a comet hits Earth, it brings Chaos: a tiny squid turned cosmic super-villain. After escaping S.O.S.I. and chugging bottled black hole juice, Chaos hunts the Dragon Crowns to rule the galaxy. Team Chaos (featuring a traitorous Darkness) vs. Team Peace (who really need to stop arguing). Can they survive a five-hour rocket ride of pure "BRUH" and stop the black hole cannon? Expect epic space battles, secret lab escapes, "predictable" villains, and endless PEEKABOOs!
