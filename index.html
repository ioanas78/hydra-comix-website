Looking for a gift idea for children? Country Balls comic books are a hit with boys and girls aged 8–17 — fun, colourful kids books made by a kid who loves comics just like them. A present idea that children actually want to read.

Country Ball Comics by HydraComix

The Country Ball series is a collection of comics for children featuring personified national flags depicted as spherical characters known as Country Balls, and experiencing chaotic adventures involving time travel, extraterrestrial invasions, and global conflicts. David Alexandru is the mastermind who has written and designed this entire crazy book series. He is also the creator of HYDRA COMIX, the brand behind all these wild adventures.
