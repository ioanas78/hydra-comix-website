About the Author

The mind behind Country Balls

The Country Balls Story

David Alexandru — a primary school boy living in the UK — is the mastermind who wrote and designed the Country Balls comic book series.

His collection of comics features personified national flags depicted as spherical characters known as Country Balls, experiencing chaotic adventures involving time travel, extraterrestrial invasions, and global conflicts.

He was 10 years old when he wrote his first comic called "Country Balls", followed by "Country Balls Return" and "Country Balls Heroes".

"Country Balls Golden Era" is a 15-chapter collection that gathers the wildest comic adventures combining the content of his three books — all in one volume.

At 11 years old he authored "Country Balls Mania".

Why Kids Love It

Throughout the series, the author utilises humour and personification to illustrate historical rivalries, sporting events, and imaginative adventures. The visual style is characterised by vibrant colours and simple dialogue bubbles to convey action and suspense within each short story.

A Gift Kids Actually Want

Looking for a present idea for children? Whether you need gift ideas for boys or gift ideas for girls, the Country Balls children's comic books are a hit with young readers aged 8–17 — bold colours, short stories, and adventures kids can actually relate to.
